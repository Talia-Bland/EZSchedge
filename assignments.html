How to Be An Effective Assignment Doer

Tip #1

Try to determine how much time will be needed to complete each assignment and be realistic!

Click Me
Tip #2

Find a friend or classmate that you can trust and explain to them that you're trying to change your homework habits. Having an accountability partner can help hold you accountable for your decisions.

Click Me
Tip #3

Create a reward system, especially if you're a procrastinator! Everyone loves an incentive. If you mix some fun into the process, you'll be more eager to get your work done.

Click Me
Tip #4

Limit your access to technology. It's all about balance overall. If an assignment doesn't require technology, what do you need your phone out for? And if you're a music listener like the rest of us, have a study playlist pre-setup, so you're not distracted looking for a song.
